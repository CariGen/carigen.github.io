What is the Caribbean Genomics (CaGe) Initiative?









CaGe is an international, multi-institutional collaboration founded in March 2020 by alumni and current professors/students of the Department of Marine Sciences at the UPR-Mayaguez with the goal of creating genomic and morphological resources for Caribbean species.

We seek to increase accessibility to the genomics world, especially for investigators beginning to work with genomic data. CaGe will accomplish this goal through the creation and sharing of educational materials and tutorials for sequence data processing and analysis and hosting workshops open to whoever is interested.
