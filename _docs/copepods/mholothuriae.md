### **General info**
*Metis holothuriae* (Edwards, 1891). The current records of *Metis holothuriae* suggest a cosmopolitan distribution, but the large amount of variation that has been reported for this species appears to reinforce the existence of a species complex (Mielke 1989; Wells 2007; Huys 2016). It is common in association to phytal and is reported to be an epibiont of the Caribbean Manatee (Violante-Huerta and E.
